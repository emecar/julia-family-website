About Me

I was born at 6:30am on September 18 2011 in Africa. We lived in a medium sized estate with a lot of friendly neighbors. I have beautiful memories of my early life in Africa, we played freely on the streets and lived like a community. Our parents worked in the same place and i went to the same school as all my neighbors. My aunts and uncles visited us a lot and my Grandmother always stayed with us. In August 2018 we moved to Canada, landed in Kingston, Ontario, before moving to Richmond BC and finally Victoria I am happy to be in canada.
